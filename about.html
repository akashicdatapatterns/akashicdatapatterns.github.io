AKASHIC Datapatterns is a leading technology company at the forefront of AI data analytics.

We specialize in leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and data science techniques to unlock valuable insights from complex datasets. Our mission is to empower businesses across industries to make smarter, data-driven decisions and drive innovation through AI..

  • Data Scientists: Our team of experienced data scientists possesses deep expertise in machine learning, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling. They are skilled in developing custom algorithms tailored to specific business needs and are adept at extracting actionable insights from large-scale datasets.
  • AI Engineers: Our AI engineers are responsible for building and deploying scalable AI solutions that drive real-world impact. They have a strong background in software engineering and are proficient in AI frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn. They work closely with data scientists to operationalize AI models and integrate them into existing systems.
  • Data Engineers: Our data engineering team is tasked with designing and maintaining robust data infrastructure and pipelines. They are experts in data warehousing, ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes, and cloud computing technologies such as AWS, Google Cloud Platform, and Azure. They ensure data quality, reliability, and accessibility for analytics and AI applications.

At AKASHIC Datapatterns, our team is our greatest asset. We foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning, where every team member is empowered to contribute their unique skills and expertise towards our common goal of driving AI-powered transformation
